Department of Computer Application

Course Details:

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is an AICTE approved Post-Graduation program in computer application spread over six semesters. This program is exclusively designed to meet the constantly evolving IT industry requirements. The fact that industries are accepting MCA students into their company is testimony to the quality of program and its credentials. MCA qualified students are working their way up to become system programmers, business analyst, system analysts and application programmers. Application areas include Software engineering, Management Information Systems, Software Quality Management, ERP, transaction processing (such as banking, stock exchange order processing) simulation, database management, e-commerce, Web enabled service, Animation, Content Development, IT Consultancy, design support and data communications, Computer networking, Data Security, embedded technologies etc.

The Students of MCA are very competent in project development as their exposure to project development is made very prominent and compulsory in the academic curriculum. During their final semester students undergo a six months training program exclusively for software development in either commercial application or systems. The project is aimed at implementing analysis, design & programming tools that have been learnt during the entire program curriculum.

VOICE OF HEAD

Welcome to the webpages of the Post Graduate Department of Computer Applications, one of the most dynamic and innovative computing fraternity in the National Capital Region of India. We are a major resource of high-quality professionals in different areas of Computer Applications. JSSATE Noida offers you a three year Full time MCA degree affiliated to Dr. APJAKTU The department faculty work with excellent team spirit in different technical team like Software Engineering, Database, Programming Languages, Networking, E-Commerce which leads to key research publications and consultancy in these areas. The alumni of the department are well placed in academia, NGOs and the development sector, and in government and private sector. The department and its faculty members conduct a regular seminar series in addition to national, and workshops. We offer the best teaching methodologies through class room and laboratory exercises and cultivate a mind-set among students to be inquisitive. We visualize being the first choice in the state/country among aspiring computer science graduates. Our students use the broad education that they receive here as a stepping stone to careers in IT industry and different institutions. The strong foundation that our students build in computer applications development makes them attractive to IT companies and institutions in a wide range of areas. Among the reasons why our students have the potential to be assets of IT industry are the strong theoretical foundation, consistent hands-on experience-based approach with emphasis on lab sessions & relevant project work guided by our faculty. In our Department you will find young, knowledgeable and dynamic faculty working along with me to transform our students into competent computer professionals besides conducting research in relevant topics. I invite you to read our web pages and find a way to become part of our extended family. We have been serving the profession for more than a decade. On behalf of my colleagues and myself, I assure you that our enthusiasm about what we do, as well as our commitment to become acknowledged leader in application-oriented areas of computing are unwavering.
